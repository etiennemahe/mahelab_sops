## Before You Start Work This protocol requires: - 70% Ethanol - Qiagen DNase booster buffer - DNase 1 stock solution (Dissolve lyophilized DNase 1 (1500 Kunitz units) in 550 μl RNase-free water) - Qiagen buffer RW1 - Qiagen buffer RPE - RNase-free water - RNeasy columns, collection and specimen tubes NOTE: DNase 1 stock solution can be kept for 9+ months at -20C Begin by _____ (# specimens being processed) x 11 μl DNase I stock solution _____ (# specimens being processed) x 77 μl Buffer RDD

0. Ensure that cell lysis steps are complete (ie buffer RLT and beta-mercaptoethanol have been added to a cell pellet/lysis, with total volume ~600 uL) 1. Add one volume (~600 uL) 70% ethanol; mix by pipetting up and down gently 2. Prepare an appropriately labelled RNeasy Column (these will come packaged with a 2 mL waste collection tube) 3. Transfer 600-700 uL of the specimen from step 1 to the column 4. Centrifuge 15 sec at 10,000 rpm 5. Discard flow-through in liquid waste 6. Repeat steps 3-5 until all the materials from step 1 are passed through the column 7. Add 350 μl Buffer RW1 to the RNeasy spin column. Close the lid gently, and centrifuge for 15 s at 10,000 rpm 8. Discard the flow-through. 9. Add 80 uL DCDWS to the column (aim directly for the membrane); let stand on the bench for 15 min. 10. Add 350 μl Buffer RW1 to the RNeasy spin column. Close the lid gently, and centrifuge for 15 s at 10,000 rpm 11. Discard flow-through 12. Add 500 μl Buffer RPE to the RNeasy spin column 13. Centrifuge for 15 s at 10,000 rpm 14. Discard flow-through 15. Repeat steps 12-14 16. Centrifuge for 2 min at 10,000 rpm to dry the membrane 17. Carefully transfer the column to a new labelled specimen tube (be sure not to get the column wet with the flow-through) 18. Carefully add 30 uL RNase-free water to the CENTER of the column. Avoid touching the walls/sides of the column with pipette tip 19. Centrifuge for 1 min at full speed to elute the RNA 20. Proceed to RNA quantification & store specimen in -20C freezer
